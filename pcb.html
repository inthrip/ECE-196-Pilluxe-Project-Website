3D View

Design Choices

  • The PCB centralizes control of LEDs and buttons so the pillbox hardware stays organized and easier to wire.
  • BLE support allows the board to communicate with the mobile app for schedules, reminders, and tracking.
  • Component placement is arranged to support a compact footprint that can fit underneath the pill containers.
  • IMU module informs when device is actually in use, optimizing power usage.

Functionality

  • Receives medication schedule data from the app through BLE to the microcontroller.
  • Activates the correct LEDs to guide the user take the correct amount of pills from the correct pill container at the scheduled time.
  • Reads button presses to confirm that the correct medication was accessed and taken.
  • Sends status updates back to the app so reminders, logs, and streak tracking stay synchronized.
