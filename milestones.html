Milestones Page

Project Milestones and Timeline

This page lays out our testable hypothesis, timeline figure, and concrete deliverables for the project. Each milestone describes completion, not when work will begin.

Testable Hypothesis

When users utilize this device to assist in taking medication, reminders remove the constant memorization and the incentivization system makes the act of taking feel rewarding rather than tedious; this ultimately improves medication adherence rates among users.

Timeline Figure

Milestones

Milestone 1 - Week 5 Friday

PCB and Connectivity Completed

PCB designed with Bluetooth capabiltiies, colored LEDs, and buttons, all fully connected.

Milestone 2 - Week 6 Friday

Software Integration Completed

Implemented software (a barebones app) that is able to communicate with the PCB and light LEDs.

Milestone 3 - Week 7 Wednesday

CAD and 3D Printing Completed

CAD model finished and the pill container 3D printed with openings for LEDs and push buttons.

Milestone 4 - Week 8 Friday

App Incentive Features Implemented

App configured with visual design, day-by-day streaks, and an incentive-based reward system.

Milestone 5 - Week 9 Monday (Optional)

Diary System Completed

Implemented diary system that enables users to track their condition while taking the medication across time.

Final Milestone - Week 9 Wednesday

Testing and Evaluation Phase Completed

Testing completed (app used as intended) to identify flaws and evaluate whether the system reduces tedium and feels enjoyable to use.
