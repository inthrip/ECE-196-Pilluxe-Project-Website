Below is an example of an existing solution, along with its main strengths and limitations.

Hero Smart Pillbox

Pros

  • Allows medication to be dispensed in a more organized and accessible way.
  • Provides reminder support through a connected app.
  • Works well for convenience and routine reminders.

Cons

  • Does not fully alleviate the tedium of long-term medication management.
  • Still leaves users responsible for refilling, organizing, and monitoring medications over time.
  • May not address more complex medication scheduling needs.
